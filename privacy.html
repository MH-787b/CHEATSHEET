Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 March 2026

What data we collect

Study Sheet collects no personal data. We do not require an account, email address, or any identifying information to use the extension.

Page content

When you click "solve this page", the extension reads the visible text content of the current webpage. This text is sent to our AI providers (Groq and Anthropic) to generate answers. The text is not stored, logged, or retained by us after the response is returned.

Local storage

The extension stores a daily usage counter in your browser's local storage to enforce the free-tier usage limit. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted.

Third-party services

Study Sheet sends page content and optional screenshots to:

  • Groq (groq.com) — for fast AI responses on straightforward questions
  • Anthropic (anthropic.com) — for accurate AI responses on complex questions

These services process the data according to their own privacy policies. No user-identifying information is included in these requests.

Payments

Premium subscriptions are handled by Gumroad (gumroad.com). We do not process or store any payment information directly. Gumroad's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions.

Children's privacy

Study Sheet does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data at all, the extension is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
